Every Thing You Want To Know

The University of Computer Studies,Yangon (UCSY) is one of the higher institutions under the Ministry of Education. UCSY is to conduct teaching and research in various branches of computer science, computer technology, and information technology.


Students both seniors and juniors can both enjoy a variety of clubs including sports and dances too. The clubs are formed by passionate students who love to show off their skills, hobbies and also share them among their friends,classmates and members.The clubs also serve as the purpose of improving the skills, practising amateur and sharping their talents.


The purpose of our website is to provide participants of our school clubs - Football, Basketball, Dance, Esports, and Table Tennis - with a centralized platform for accessing information, communicating with club members and leaders, accessing resources, coordinating events, and fostering a sense of community within each club.
