Purpose of club

Our club's purpose is to provide a platform for students who are interested in dancing to come together and enjoy this hobby in a social and to explore different styles of dance, improve skills, and connect with other members who share the interests. It can serve as a way for students to meet new people, make friends, and maintain physical fitness while having fun.The club may offer classes or workshops for members to learn different dance styles and techniques and attend university festivals such as fresher welcome and Thingyan.
