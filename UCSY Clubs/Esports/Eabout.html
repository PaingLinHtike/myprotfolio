About Us

As a representative establishment of the Esports field of our University, we provide and support our members with best facility. Weekly scrims, Friendly matches with other University, fun activities and many other fun things to create a better environment for the community.

Goals and Priorities

To create a better Gaming Community and raise the rising eSports stars is our number one priority. Compete in tournaments, win trophies and to stand out at the top of Myanmar Esports is our Ultimate goals. With Esports and Gaming, we will raise our University and Nation's pride and make the world calling out for our names.
