History and Establishement

UCSY Gaming and Esports club is the club stands for the Gaming Esports community of UCSY. With the agreement of rule and regulation with the UCSY student's Union, on 23rd January 2019, UCSY Gaming and Esports club is established. Our club promise to bring the best Gaming Experiences, tournaments for the students and become an establishment that can raise th e University's pride!!
