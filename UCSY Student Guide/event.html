Fresher Welcome

"Coming together is a beginning"

A Fresher's Welcome in UCSY is a truly exciting event that you won t want to miss. It's not just about the fun and games, it's about becoming part of a community.
From ice-breaking games that encourage interaction, to cultural performances that celebrate diversity, there's something for everyone. You might find yourself participating in school's drama, showcasing your singing, or even dancing talents. Each activity is designed to create a memorable experience and promote unity among the freshers.
So, come and embrace this wonderful opportunity. The Fresher's Welcome is more than just an event, it's the start of an exciting academic journey. It's a celebration of new beginnings and a testament to the welcoming spirit of the school. So, get ready to make some unforgettable memories and make sure to have fun. If you are a still a junior, then welcome aboard!


Other exciting festivals and competitions

Thingyan Festival

Dive into the fun by participating in water-throwing, cultural dances, and tasting traditional foods. A unique experience filled with laughter and excitement!

"Hta-Ma-Ne" Competition

Participate to experience the delightful blend of tradition and fun all while enjoying the lively festivities. It's a cultural immersion like no other!

New Year Festival

It's a time of fun, laughter, and camaraderie, where we bid farewell to the old year and welcome the new with our friends. Don't miss out on this wonderful celebration!

Dhamma Competition

An enriching experience that deepens our understanding of Dhamma, fostering a sense of respect and appreciation for our cultural heritage.

Farewell Party

A bittersweet celebration that marks the end of each semester. It's a mix of fun and nostalgia, where we celebrate our achievements, bid farewell to friends, and look forward to new beginnings.

Rainy Season Competitions

A thrilling event filled with a variety of contests. It's a fun-filled opportunity for everyone to showcase their talents and compete for the top prize. Don't miss out on this exciting chance to shine!
